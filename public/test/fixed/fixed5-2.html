Product availability

Received the order and before the shipment, Pickyshop.com proceeds to the quality control and validation by email of the order processing. If the ordered items are no longer available or they have not passed quality control, we will give immediate notice to the customer, proposing alternatives.

Processing orders

Your order will be processed as soon as payment has been received, within 48 hours by a positive result of the payment. Pickyshop.com reserves the right to refuse the orders to anyone for any reason.

Shipping methods

Pickyshop.com shipments are insured against theft and accidental damage. Once the shipment reaches the destination, the goods are no longer insured.

Delivery

Items that are already available will be shipped in 1-2 business days (monday-friday) following order completion

Orders placed over the weekend will be processed on the following Monday.

Orders will be processed within 48 hours of receipt of payment for orders received by 11:30 CET.

We cannot ship to parcel shop.
